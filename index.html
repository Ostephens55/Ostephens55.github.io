About Me

Hello, my name is Owen Stephens and I am currently a student-athlete at Babson College. I chose to come to Babson for a number of reasons. The first being that I am a baseball player and the program here is very competitive for the Division III level. The opportunity to continue playing at a high level, while also being part of a supportive and driven team, was something that really appealed to me. I also chose Babson because it is an excellent business school and I knew long before I arrived here that I had a keen interest in studying business at the university level. Babson’s focus on entrepreneurship and practical learning was something that stood out to me, as I wanted to be in an environment that would not only challenge me academically but also provide real-world applications for the knowledge I would gain. The combination of athletics and academics made Babson the ideal place for me to grow both personally and professionally.

I am currently in my junior year at Babson, and I have thoroughly enjoyed my experience so far, both on the field and in the classroom. Over my two and a half years here, I have had the opportunity to grow academically, socially, and personally, and through these experiences, I have decided to concentrate in Finance. This decision is the first step in reaching my goal of working in the wealth management industry, a field that excites me due to its potential for making a meaningful impact in people’s lives. I believe this industry is a great fit for me because it is incredibly client-focused, and I am drawn to the idea of building strong, trust-based relationships while helping individuals secure their financial futures. The opportunity to work closely with clients and guide them toward financial security resonates deeply with me, and I’m excited to develop the skills necessary to succeed in this dynamic and rewarding field.

My Interests

I have a number of hobbies outside of Baseball. The first being golf, I enjoy playing golf because of the challenging nature of the sport. It is extremely difficult to master and there is no better feeling than hitting a ball flush. It also allows me to spend quality outdoor time with friends and family. Other than golf, my favorite hobby is fishing. It requires a lot of patience and time commitment, with most trips resulting in no catch. But regardless of our success or lack thereof, I enjoy every minute I spend on the water with my friends and family. There is a certain element of the ocean that is calming to me, it is truly my happy place.
